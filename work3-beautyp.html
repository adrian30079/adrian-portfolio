Creating Persona

After we collected and neatened our research data, Insights, the Needs and the Pain Points are uncovered from it. We created two personas to depict Watsons beauty product customers.

Phrase 2: Workshop

Heading to idealise the enhanced feature, a team is formed to provide and consolidate features based on our created personas.

Assumption Mapping

With personas pain points and goals were established, assumption mappings are made to explicite and poioritise desirability, viability, and feasibility hypotheses in terms of importance.

After hypotheses are explicited, they are paired up with business goals, user goals, and technical considerations. By comparing 3 aspects of consideration, It leads us to map out the overlapping areas where identified the project direction.

Define Questions

Assumption mapping inights are translated into sets of user goal groupings. Then participants generated How Might We questions targeting on each grouping.

Voting

After HMW questions were placed, we entered a voting section where we selected 5 HMW question for further sketches and layout.

Brainstorming

To idealize and skeletonise the overall look on each selected HMW question, we organized a Crazy 8 section to do that.

Phrase 3: Visualisation
