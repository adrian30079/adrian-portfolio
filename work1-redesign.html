Web Redesign
(Homepage)
As other sub-brands start renewing their website. Website redesign is requested by the head and UX team help reviewing and gathering the problem of our existing website.
Role
✧Research
✧UI Design
✧Proposal drafting
Proposed Person
✧Marketing Head
✦Research and Problem observed

Attension mapping is used to obsereve an overall picture on user behaviour.

Scroll heat map
💡 Content and words overwhelmed in navigation bar
💡 Low readability on the CTA button to control the banne
💡 Hero banner is covering fairly large proportion of first screen
💡 Homepage content are wasted and contradicts to the original intention that user would use and browse
Cursor movement heat map
💡 High usage on vital function such as Shopping bag, Account info and search bar
💡 Navigation highlights are indicated low attention
💡 Function can be rearranged in a more organised order
Questionnaire

Targeting navigation content, tasks are given to 500 persons to find 10 designated products. Tree test information architecture is collected from over 500 persons.

Example case:
How would user find a bag of Watsons Square Puffs?

Tree test insight
💡 Users are facing trouble in differentiating “Brands” & “Watson Brands” & “Top Brands” on navigation level1.
💡 Exculsive grouping is confusing as content set from marketing oriented content
💡 “Brand” and “Watson brand” are both effective in product searching
GA observation

By observating the source of entrance in targeted links, I found an issue of low direct rate from our original page.
Also relatively low page view time is found.

Example case:
Finding source of entry in navigation tabs?

Other observed problems

Mobile web Navigation content is not aligned to Website content, and the Mobile navigation format is different from New App format,
which may causes inconsistent browsing experience when customer shops online.

✦Initial design

1. Aligned mobile navigation sequence with App
2. By minimizing hero banner and simplified navigation content, landing page is more broswable
3. In expansed navigation, marketing tags and image are added to filfilling both user and marketing needs.
